FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is Chesscord? Chesscord is a Discord bot that allows users to play Chess against each other, solve puzzles, do game reviews, customize their experience, and more.
How can I play chess on Chesscord? To play chess on Chesscord, simply invite the bot to your discord server and type '/play' to start a game.
Can I customize the appearance of Chesscord? Yes, Chesscord allows you to customize various aspects of the bot's appearance, such as board themes, piece styles, and more.
Is Chesscord suitable for beginners? Absolutely! Chesscord provides features like puzzles and game reviews that can help beginners improve their Chess skills.
How can I report a bug or suggest a feature for Chesscord? If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions for new features, please join our support server and let us know. We appreciate your feedback!
